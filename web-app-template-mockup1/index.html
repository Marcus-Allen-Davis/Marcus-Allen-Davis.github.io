About Me

Hey there! My names Marcus Davis. I was born in Montana and raised in Texas. I graduated from high school a few months early and served a US Navy contract as an AO. During my time in I found and deepened a love for technology and how it can be used. I started with HTML, CSS and then fell into the Python rabbit hole and loved it. After my contract was up with the Navy I moved to L.A. to pursue broadening my horizons and experiencing more of life. At my first job I got connected to an old Grip who got me a job throwing rags and setting up small-time gigs. I was hooked instantly with the idea of all these vastly creative and hardworking people bringing dreams to life. That moment on after meeting others in different disciplines I knew I wanted to work with VFX. I took a few online paid courses for Houdini simulations, Maya modeling and lighting systems but man compositing hooked me as soon as I started. Something about putting together all the beautiful work others have done and combining it all into a cohesive whole is something between Alchemy and Magic! This leaves me wanting to continue on my path and make great things with even greater people!
